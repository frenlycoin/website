About Frenly App And Frenly Coin

Frenly App (@FrenlyRobot) is a TON mining game that sells ads on its channel and distributes earnings to its users to keep TON users informed and engaged and reward them for it.

Overview

It creates new TON by many different means:

  • selling premium accounts
  • selling native Telegram ads on the channel
  • selling ads on the channel directly
  • selling ads on our other social networks
  • lottery
  • TON staking
  • selling other perks in the app

Each user receives 0.01 FREN (Frenly Energy) for free and to start mining, he has to subscribe to @FrenlyNews channel, channel for publishing ads and news about TON ecosystem and projects in it.

Users mine FREN and they can either withdraw their rewards any time they want or compound them to increase their earnings. Users can also mint new FREN by using TON. When withdrawing, FREN is automatically converted to TON.

In last phase, FRCO (Frenly Coin) will become a token on TON network.

Project Milestones

Planned development phases for the Frenly ecosystem.

0. Phase 0

Starting phase in which we develop basic Telegram mini app with a referral system, basic mining, withdrawal, channel subscription check and other basic MVP (Minimum Viable Product) functionality. Price of minting FREN in this phase is 0.1 TON.

0.5. Phase 0.5

This assumes finished phase 0 and start of subtle advertising. Price of minting FREN in this phase is 0.25 TON.

1. Phase 1

Develop features for daily mining cycle to incentivize users for bigger engagement on the channel, to stay informed and interested in TON ecosystem. Price of minting FREN in this phase is 0.5 TON.

1.5. Phase 1.5

Finished phase 1 and start advertising more seriously. Price of minting FREN in this phase is 1 TON.

2. Phase 2

We expand our advertising network from just Telegram to X, Facebook and possibly other to increase earnings for the network by selling ads on those social networks too. Price of minting FREN in this phase is 1 TON.

3. Phase 3

Build the Frenly Coin token on TON network. Price of minting FREN in this phase is 1 TON.
